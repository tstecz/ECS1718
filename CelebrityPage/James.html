Lebron James

Early Life

Lebron Raymone James was born on December 30, 1984 in Akron OH. He was raised in a single parent family by his mother, Gloria James. They often lived in not so great neihborhoods in Akron. For High School, Lebron James attended St. Vincent St. Mary High School. He played basketball and football there.

NBA Career

Lebron has played for the Miami Heat and the Cleveland Cavaliers. He has won the championship three times, and has been in it six times. He has 29650 career points. He has also played 1092 games. This means that he has a career average of 27 points per game.
