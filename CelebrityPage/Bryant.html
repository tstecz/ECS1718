Kris Bryant

Early Life

Kris Bryant was born on January 4th, 1992. He was from Las Vegas, Nevada. He attended Bonanza High School, in Las Vegas, and played baseball there. He had a .418 batting average. He attended University of San Diego, played baseball, and had a .365 batting average as a freshman! He also go All-American in his Sohpmore year!

Career

In 2013, Bryant was drafted by the cubs. He played for the Boise Hawks and the Daytona Cubs in the minor leagues. He also played with the Tenessee Smokies and the Iowa Cubs. In 2015, Bryant was called up to the Majors, and won the World Series in 2016!
